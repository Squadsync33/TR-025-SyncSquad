#JobShield AI – Fake Job Detection System

JobShield AI is an intelligent job verification system designed to detect fraudulent job postings using Artificial Intelligence, NLP techniques, and real-time risk analysis.

This system helps users identify suspicious job opportunities before applying, reducing the risk of job scams and financial fraud.

---

#Features

AI-Based Job Analysis  
Analyzes job descriptions using Natural Language Processing (NLP).

Fake Job Risk Detection  
Detects suspicious patterns such as payment requests, unrealistic salary, and unknown recruiters.

Risk Score Prediction  
Displays probability of job being fake (Low / Medium / High Risk).

 Email Authenticity Check  
Identifies suspicious email domains like Gmail, Yahoo, etc.

 Salary Benchmark Comparison  
Compares salary offered with real market benchmarks.

 Explainable AI (XAI)  
Shows reasons why a job is flagged as suspicious.

 Job History Storage  
Stores analyzed jobs using Supabase database.

Mobile-Friendly UI  
Built using Flutter with Android-friendly responsive design.
